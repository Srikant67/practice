Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. It was released for Android, iOS, PlayStation 4, and Windows in 2020, on PlayStation 5 in 2021, and is set for release on Nintendo Switch.

The game features an anime-style open-world environment and an action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching.Genshin Impact takes place in the fantasy world of Teyvat, home to seven nations, each of which is tied to a different element and ruled by a different god. The story follows the Traveler, who has traveled across countless worlds with their twin sibling before becoming separated in Teyvat.

Mondstadt

Mondstadt is one of the seven regions in Teyvat, and the first in which the Traveler starts to look for their lost sibling. It is the city-state that worships Lord Barbatos, the Anemo Archon.

Ever since the beginning, Mondstadt has celebrated a festival called the Ludi Harpastum every year, with 15 days of games for the children to enjoy.

Liyue

Liyue is the city-state in the world of Teyvat that worships Rex Lapis, the Geo Archon.

The nation of Liyue is marked by its emphasis on "contracts," Morax's ideal. It is said that people from all seven nations of Teyvat flock to Liyue Harbor, the economic hub of the continent.

Inazuma

Inazuma is an archipelagic region that worships Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon, who is also the leader of its governing body, the Inazuma Shogunate. Inazuma is located approximately 4 km southeast from Liyue Harbor and is a close neighbor with the Dark Sea.

In the past year relative to the current events of the game, the situation in Inazuma has made a drastic turn towards isolationism, locked down under the Sakoku Decree. "Baal" also enacted the related Vision Hunt Decree, declaring that Visions, as gifts from the gods, should be in the sole hands of divinity — and is now rounding up all Visions in Inazuma to inlay them in the hands of a statue of the Thousand-Armed, Hundred-Eyed God.

Sumeru

Sumeru is the nation in Teyvat that worships Lesser Lord Kusanali, the Dendro Archon.

It is renowned as the center of learning and houses the Sumeru Akademiya.

Fontaine

Fontaine is one of the seven regions in Teyvat. It is the city-state that worships the God of Justice, also known as focalors the Hydro Archon.

As of patch 3.5, the nation of Fontaine is yet to be released.

Natlan

Natlan is one of the seven regions in Teyvat. It is the city-state that worships Murata, the Pyro Archon.

As of patch 3.5, the nation of Natlan is yet to be released.

Snezhnaya

Snezhnaya is one of the seven regions of Teyvat. Those who live in Snezhnaya worship the Cryo Archon, the Tsaritsa — and the Fatui, a religion/military-force under the Tsaritsa's control, is particularly notorious for their diehard devotion to her and their unscrupulous methods in enacting her will.

As of patch 3.5, the nation of Snezhnaya is yet to be released.
